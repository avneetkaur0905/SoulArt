About Me

The Artist

A creator at heart, finding stillness through the lens and the brush.

Art is my essential escape from a fast-paced world. When I’m sketching or painting, the noise of daily life fades away, and everything feels beautifully slow and calm. It’s a meditative space where I can explore ideas without constraints.

Parallel to that, photography allows me to pause and appreciate the world around me. I love capturing fleeting, beautiful moments—freezing a feeling in time that might otherwise be missed. For me, it’s all about finding the beauty in the little things and sharing how I see them.
